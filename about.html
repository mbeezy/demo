About DemoSurfboards.com

What is Demo Surfboards?

DemoSurfboards.com is your online destintation to demo surfboards from surfers and shapers in San Diego, Del Mar, Encinitas, Carlsbad and Solana Beach.

Why Demo Surfboards?

Looking for one place to demo surfboards from local shapers?

We feature surfboards from local San Diego shapers for you to try out.

Incoming swell in the forecast and need a step-up? No problem.

San Diego doesn't get big swell often so instead of buying a step-up that you're only going to use a couple of times a year, demo a surfboard instead.

Going out of town but don't want to travel with your surfboard? Demo someone else's while you're in town.

Traveling with surfing equipment is a pain. Demo a surfboard instead to save you the hassle of flying with your stuff.

Looking to sell your surfboard? Try demo'ing it out.

Instead of selling your surfboard for lower than it's really worth, demo your surfboard instead to get recurring cash.
